My Story

Being in the midst of a career change evokes strange feelings: I feel simultaneously excited and sure, as well as daunted and not sure at all. I take this as a good sign. Feeling such about a career path is new to me. I have travelled a few paths already, and never felt this wanting.

I have always known I wanted to make a difference in people’s lives, no matter which path I took. From working with the elderly and handicapped to guiding people through yoga practices to making all-natural and non-toxic cleaning solutions from scratch for my own small-business cleaning local homes… I always had this goal in mind.

So here I am, nearly a year into my journey and eager to contribute again to improving people’s quality of life, this time through design. I have always had a knack with creativity and art, and thanks to CareerFoundry’s UI Immersion course, I am able to apply this knack to technology to produce aesthetic and effective design solutions for real problems.

Another, more selfish perk that this path brings is the opportunity to constantly grow and learn. Learning has been a hobby of mine ever since I can remember. I can already see that I will never reach the finish line with design, which is wonderful. The industry is ever-changing and evolving and is full to the brim of creative and inspiring minds that I can’t wait to learn from.

Last, but not least, it’s fun! Having fun is not just pleasing to me, but really allows me to put my all into my work day in and day out.

My design style is adaptable as I strive to maintain a beginner’s mindset so that I don’t become stuck while the rest of the design realm continues to shift and grow. I’m the type of person that wants to know the “why” behind things, and asks many questions along the way. I am eager to try my hand at freelancing, contributing to start-ups, or working for a company if the fit is right, so please feel free to reach out, and I will get back to you soon.

Thank you for taking the time to read about my story.
