Welcome!
I'm Jordanya le Fae. I'm a UX/UI Designer from Asheville, NC.

I’m a Visual Designer with an aim to improve people’s quality of life via my design skills. I come from a background of working in the healthcare industry with the elderly, teaching yoga, and owning and operating an all-natural house cleaning business. Connecting with people in ways that uplift them is the common thread in my story.
